## Q3: How is Temu's business model restructuring post-de minimis?

## Q4: What do Heimao complaint patterns reveal about platform quality and merchant treatment?

**This is genuinely strong bottom-up data that no earnings call discusses.** The Taobao <10% response rate and Douyin's 99% rate is a surprising finding.

## Q5: How is Douyin's shift from livestream to shelf commerce progressing?

**Status:** ANSWERED

**Evidence Summary:**
- 2 evidence packets, 8 findings
- Primary source %: ~40% (mostly 36kr/kr-asia, secondary)

**Key Answer:**
- Shelf commerce: 30% (2023) -> 40% (2024) -> 50% (mid-2025 618), on track with 3-year plan
- Shelf GMV growing 49% YoY vs 34% overall Douyin e-commerce growth
- Total GMV: ~3.5T yuan (2024), targeting 4.2T (2025), approaching Pinduoduo scale (~44% of Taobao/Tmall)
- Category strength: Douyin captured 26% of apparel during Double 11 2025 (Tmall 57.5%, JD 17%)
- Merchant subsidies: 32B yuan in total cost savings for 2025, including commission waivers and promotion fee refunds
- Ocean Engine (advertising arm) merged into Douyin E-commerce in March 2025
- Influencer dependence declining: store-hosted streams >30%, influencer streams ~30% (down from majority)

**Gaps:** ByteDance does not disclose revenue/profit. No primary source for category-level GMV splits. Competitive response from Taobao/PDD not well documented.
