**What This Reveals:** Temu's cross-border logistics network, built under tariff duress, may ultimately be a stronger competitive asset than the de minimis arbitrage it replaced. Pre-positioned inventory enables 1-3 day delivery (vs. 7-15 days), which is the single biggest driver of consumer retention.
