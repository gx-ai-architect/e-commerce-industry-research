## 7. Key Competitive Themes for PDD/Temu

### 7.1 Threats
1. **Tariff headwinds**: De minimis elimination hit Temu hardest (-52% US DAU); forces shift to local warehousing and semi-managed logistics
2. **Alibaba resurgence**: AIDC turned profitable; AliExpress Choice at 70%+ of orders; RMB 380B AI/cloud investment
3. **TikTok Shop's explosive growth**: $64.3B GMV (+94%); $15.1B in US alone — emerging as primary competitor in social commerce
4. **JD.com user surge**: 700M+ active users; food delivery creating new engagement loops; Joybuy entering Europe
5. **Amazon's defensive moats**: $830B GMV; Haul expanding to 25 countries; seller pressure tactics against Temu

### 7.2 Opportunities
1. **Scale advantage in cross-border**: Temu matched Amazon at 24% cross-border market share — from near-zero in 2022
2. **Shein weakness**: IPO delays, supply chain scrutiny, valuation collapse ($100B to $30B); Temu growing faster in non-fashion categories
3. **Douyin convergence**: Douyin approaching PDD-level GMV in China (RMB 4T+) but not a direct cross-border competitor
4. **EU expansion window**: Before July 2026 EU duty kicks in, Temu can build European warehouse infrastructure
5. **Price sensitivity remains**: Despite tariffs, consumer demand for low-cost goods persists — 40% of Americans say they'll pay more for US-made, but behavior lags intent
