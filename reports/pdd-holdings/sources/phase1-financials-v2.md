## 3. Temu / Tariff Impact

### De Minimis Exemption Ended (2025-05-02)
- U.S. ended de minimis exemption for goods from China on May 2, 2025
- Temu daily active users (DAUs) fell ~52-58% in May vs March 2025 (from ~70M to under 30M)
- Monthly active users (MAUs) down 30% (Sensor Tower) to 46% (Bloomberg Intelligence) in April-June period
- App Store ranking collapsed from avg top 3 to rank ~132 by May 2025
- Mscience analyst Vinci Zhang: "massive bottom line miss is due to much weaker than expected operating margin, likely impacted by US tariffs"
[DATE: 2025-05-02 to 2025-06-30]
Source: [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/news/ecommerce/2025/report-temu-loses-58-of-us-daily-users-due-to-tariffs/), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/06/05/shein-temu-see-us-demand-plunge-on-de-minimis-trade-loophole-closure.html), [HyperSKU](https://www.hypersku.com/v2/temu-usa-and-the-de-minimis-loophole-2025/)

### Temu Recovery and Pivot
- By August 2025, Temu "quickly recovered" from the sharp sales decline
- Shifted logistics: building U.S. warehouse network (projected 15-20% of US volume in 2025, 20-25% by 2026)
- Global MAUs reached record 530 million in August 2025
- Cumulative global downloads exceeded 1.2 billion by October 2025
- Non-U.S. users rose to 90% of 405M global MAUs in Q2 (HSBC data)
- European expansion provided 80-100% GMV increase, but EU de minimis phase-out in 2026 threatens replication of U.S. challenges
- One Big Beautiful Bill Act (signed July 2025) will end de minimis exception for all countries by 2027
[DATE: 2025-08 to 2025-10]
Source: [Chinascope](https://chinascope.org/archives/37862), [StockTwits/ainvest](https://www.ainvest.com/news/temu-viability-post-de-minimis-era-strategic-reassessment-investors-2512/), [Morning Consult](https://pro.morningconsult.com/analysis/de-minimis-temu-shein-tariffs-2025)

## 7. Key Changes Since Mid-2025 (Summary)

1. **Revenue growth deceleration:** From +24% in Q4 2024 to +10% / +7% / +9% in Q1-Q3 2025
2. **Profitability reset:** Operating margins compressed dramatically (33% -> 19% non-GAAP in Q1 2025) due to merchant subsidies and ecosystem investments; some stabilization by Q3
3. **Temu U.S. collapse and recovery:** Lost 52-58% of U.S. DAUs post-de minimis removal (May 2025), but recovered by August; pivoted to U.S. warehousing model
4. **Global Temu growth:** Record 530M MAUs globally (Aug 2025), 1.2B cumulative downloads; non-US now 90% of users
5. **Cash pile growing:** From RMB 332B (Dec 2024) to RMB 387B (Jun 2025) -- ~US$54B with zero capital returns
6. **RMB 100B merchant support program** launched, depressing near-term margins
7. **Stock de-rating:** Trading at ~10.5x trailing P/E, well below peer averages; price ~$103-107 vs 52-week high of $139
8. **EU de minimis risk:** EU phase-out in 2026 could replicate U.S. challenges; One Big Beautiful Bill Act ends exception globally by 2027
9. **Q4 2025 results imminent:** March 25, 2026; EPS estimate $3.04
