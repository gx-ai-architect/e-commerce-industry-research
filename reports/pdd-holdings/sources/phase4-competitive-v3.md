### Amazon Haul — Response to Temu

- Launched November 2024, mobile-only, all products under $20 (most under $10)
- $3.99 shipping fee, waived over $25; 1-2 week delivery (similar to Temu)
- 5% off orders >$50, 10% off >$75
- Ships from Amazon's Chinese warehouses under A-to-Z Guarantee
- **Adoption struggles**: Only 16% of Americans use Haul monthly vs 28% Temu, 23% Shein (Omnisend survey, early 2025). [DATE: 2025-Q1]
- CEO Jassy called it "off to a very strong start" despite data showing slow adoption. [DATE: 2025-02]
- Key vulnerability: relies on de minimis exemption (now eliminated in both US and EU)

Source: [Retail Dive](https://www.retaildive.com/news/amazon-haul-shopper-behavior-competition-shein-temu/742025/), [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2024/11/13/amazon-haul-temu-shein-discount-store-tiktok-shop/)

### Key Takeaways vs PDD

- Amazon International growing 13% vs Temu's explosive growth, but from a much larger base ($162B vs ~$71B GMV)
- Amazon Haul is a direct competitive response but struggling to gain traction
- Amazon's logistics advantage (same-day/next-day) doesn't apply to Haul (1-2 week delivery)
- De minimis elimination hurts both Amazon Haul and Temu equally
- Amazon investing in "quick commerce" and sharper pricing in international stores for 2026

### Combined Douyin/TikTok Shop Threat Assessment

- **International**: TikTok Shop's explosive US growth (407% -> 108%) creates a new competitive vector. Unlike Temu's "search and browse" model, TikTok Shop uses algorithmic content discovery.
- **Key differentiator**: TikTok/Douyin monetizes attention (ad + commerce); PDD monetizes intent (search + price comparison).

## 4.6 EU Regulatory

### DSA Enforcement

- [DATE: 2024-10-31] EU Commission opened formal proceedings against Temu under DSA. Source: [EC Press](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/ip_25_1913)
- [DATE: 2025-07-29] Commission **preliminarily found Temu in breach of DSA** Article 34 — inadequate risk assessment for illegal products. Mystery shopping exercise found consumers "very likely" to encounter non-compliant products (baby toys, electronics). Source: [EC Digital Strategy](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/news/commission-preliminarily-finds-temu-breach-digital-services-act-relation-illegal-products-its)
- Ongoing investigation into: addictive design features, recommendation system transparency, researcher data access
- **Potential fine**: Up to 6% of global annual turnover

### Foreign Subsidies Regulation (FSR) — Dublin Raid

- [DATE: 2025-12-10] EU Commission raided Temu's European HQ at 25 St Stephen's Green, Dublin 2, Ireland. Source: [Irish Times](https://www.irishtimes.com/business/2025/12/10/eu-regulators-raid-temus-dublin-facility-over-concerns-of-chinese-state-subsidies/), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-12-10/temu-s-europe-hq-raided-as-part-of-eu-foreign-subsidy-probe)
- Second-ever FSR dawn raid (first was April 2024)
- Investigation concerns: whether Chinese state subsidies (interest-free loans, tax breaks, preferential treatment) enable Temu to undercut European competitors
- Case remains at preliminary stage; no in-depth investigation formally opened yet
- **Potential penalty**: Up to 10% of global annual turnover
- **Legal precedent**: Court confirmed Commission can request data stored on servers outside EU, including in China

### De Minimis Threshold Elimination

- [DATE: 2025-11-13] European Council voted to eliminate the EUR 150 de minimis customs duty exemption. Source: [EC Taxation & Customs](https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu/news/e-commerce-150-eur-customs-duty-exemption-threshold-be-removed-2026-2025-11-13_en)
- **July 1, 2026**: Interim phase — EUR 3 flat-rate customs duty per consignment
- **2028**: Full implementation — normal customs tariffs on all goods (pending EU customs data hub)
- In 2024: ~4.6 billion parcels entered EU under de minimis, >90% from China (2x prior year volume)
- US also eliminated $800 de minimis on Aug 29, 2025; UK plans to remove GBP 135 relief by March 2029

### 2026 Regulatory Outlook

- Commissioner McGrath announced two major 2026 initiatives: revamped CPC Regulation for stronger cross-border enforcement, and new **Digital Fairness Act** targeting dark patterns, addictive design, unfair personalized advertising. Source: [Irish Times](https://www.irishtimes.com/technology/big-tech/2026/01/05/eu-readies-tougher-tech-enforcement-in-2026-as-trump-warns-of-retaliation/)
- EESC: 12 million parcels/day valued under EUR 150 shipped to EU consumers from platforms like Temu and Shein. Source: [EESC](https://www.eesc.europa.eu/en/news-media/press-releases/europe-not-sale-civil-society-and-authorities-join-forces-counter-unfair-competition-temu-and-shein)
- Temu has ~116M average monthly users in EU despite launching only in April 2023
