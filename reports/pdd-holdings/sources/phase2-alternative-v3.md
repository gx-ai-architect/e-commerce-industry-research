## 6. Regulatory Timeline

### US: De Minimis / Section 321 Reform

**Background:**
- De minimis entries exploded from 134M (2015) to 1.36B annually (2024) — driven by Temu, Shein, AliExpress [DATE: 2024-12-31]
  - Source: CBP data via https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/R48380

**Biden Administration (Late 2024 - Early 2025):**
- Jan 2025: Two proposed rulemakings requiring HTSUS numbers for Section 321 entries and tariffs on goods subject to Sections 201, 232, 301 [DATE: 2025-01-31]
- Sep 2024: Biden statement on "significant increased abuse of the de minimis exemption" [DATE: 2024-09-13]
  - Source: https://natlawreview.com/article/white-house-and-ustr-announce-changes-de-minimis-exception-and-section-301-tariffs

**Trump Administration Escalation:**
- **May 2, 2025**: De minimis exemption suspended for China and Hong Kong — 85% drop in low-value parcel volume (4M/day to 600K/day) [DATE: 2025-05-02]
- **Aug 29, 2025**: Global de minimis exemption ended for ALL countries (closes rerouting backdoor) [DATE: 2025-08-29]
  - Source: https://blogs.tradlinx.com/global-de-minimis-exemption-ends-suddenly-the-biggest-u-s-trade-shock-since-section-301/

**Phased Duty Structure:**
- Phase 1 (Aug 29, 2025 - Feb 28, 2026): Flat per-item duty $80-$200/item, varies by country [DATE: 2025-08-29]
- Phase 2 (Starting Mar 1, 2026): Full ad valorem tariffs based on value and country-of-origin tariff schedule [DATE: 2026-03-01]
  - Source: https://www.stord.com/reports/de-minimis-guide

**Tariff Rates:**
- Baseline: 10% tariff on formerly duty-free shipments [DATE: 2025-08-29]
- Apparel: avg 23.8% rate [DATE: 2025-08-29]
- Most Chinese imports: up to 145% total tariffs [DATE: 2025-08-29]
  - Source: https://www.whitecase.com/insight-alert/united-states-begins-restrain-cross-border-e-commerce

**Legislative Activity:**
- One, Big, Beautiful Bill Act signed July 4 (originally set global de minimis end for July 1, 2027 — timeline was accelerated) [DATE: 2025-07-04]
- Import Security and Fairness Act proposed in 2023-2024 Congress (would exclude China/Russia from de minimis) [DATE: 2024-12-31]
  - Source: https://www.freightamigo.com/en/blog/logistics/section-321-understanding-the-latest-changes-to-de-minimis-shipments/
