## 2. GMV Triangulation (4 Methods)

### Method 1: Revenue / Take Rate Back-Calculation

- FY2024 revenue RMB 393.84B (XBRL). Estimated domestic Pinduoduo revenue: ~RMB 213-244B. [PKT-GMV-002]
- Domestic take rate: 4.5-5.5% (analyst consensus). Domestic GMV = RMB 3.9-5.4T. [PKT-GMV-002]
- Temu GMV: ~$50B+ (~RMB 360B+). Total: **RMB 4.3-5.9T**. Confidence: Medium-High. [PKT-GMV-002]

### Method 2: Logistics Back-Calculation

- China 2025 total: ~199B parcels. PDD estimated 25-30% share: 50-60B parcels/year. [PKT-GMV-003]
- Adjusted per-parcel GMV: RMB 75-96 (consistent with 2-3 items at RMB 30-50 each). [PKT-GMV-003]
- ZTO data point: 38.52B parcels (19.4% of industry). If ZTO's PDD exposure ~40-50%, that's 15-19B PDD parcels through ZTO alone. [PKT-GMV-003]
- Domestic GMV: **RMB 3.6-5.4T**. Confidence: Medium. [PKT-GMV-003]

### Method 3: Third-Party Estimates

- ECDB ranks Pinduoduo at **$817B GMV** in 2025 (#2 globally, behind Amazon $845B, ahead of Douyin $648B). [PKT-GMV-004]
- Analyst consensus for domestic PDD GMV: **RMB 4.5-4.8T** ($620-660B). [PKT-GMV-004]
- Temu: crossed $50B in 2025, H1 alone ~$35B. ECDB reports $92.5B (may be GMV not net revenue). [PKT-GMV-004]
- Total: **RMB 5.0-6.3T**. Confidence: Medium-High. [PKT-GMV-004]

### Method 4: App Transaction Proxy (MAU x Frequency x AOV)

- Pinduoduo app MAU: 540.57M (iiMedia Aug 2025). Likely undercounts WeChat mini-program users. [PKT-GMV-005]

## 4. Temu vs Amazon vs Shein

### Price Comparison (Pre-Tariff, Omnisend Study Dec 2024 - Jan 2025)

- 77% of Amazon products have a close match on Temu. 10% are identical. [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Close-match products: **40% cheaper on Temu** (saving ~$13.37/product). [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Identical branded products: **only 1% cheaper** on Temu. [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Fashion category: 55% cheaper on Temu, 94% match rate. [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Automotive: the ONLY category where Amazon beats Temu (12% cheaper). [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Camera/photo: 96% match rate but 0% same brand — heavy brand imitation/generic alternatives. [PKT-PRICE-004]

### Trust Gap

- 87% of Americans trust Amazon vs **only 5% trust Temu**. [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Amazon: 50,000 reviews/listing average vs Temu: 1,500/listing. [PKT-PRICE-004]
- Amazon launched "Amazon Haul" (sub-$20 products) as direct competitive response to Temu. [PKT-PRICE-004]

### Post-Tariff Impact (May 2025+)

- Temu added 130-150% import charges on China-shipped items. Examples: dress $18.47 + $26.21 charge = $44.68; handheld vacuum $16.93 + $21.68 = $40.11. [PKT-PRICE-003]
- Temu US MAU collapsed 54%: ~34M (May 2024) -> <8M (May 2025). App Store ranking fell from top 10 to #73. [PKT-PRICE-003]
- US consumer spending on Temu fell ~36% in May 2025. [PKT-LOG-DEMINIMIS-001]

### Temu US Comeback (September 2025)

- Cut prices 18% on average (up to 60%) on best-selling US products. [PKT-PRICE-005]
- Resumed advertising aggressively: from "a few dozen" daily ads (Q2) to "thousands or 10,000+" (September). [PKT-PRICE-005]
- Shein captured 45% of fast-fashion app market share during Temu's retreat, motivating the comeback. [PKT-PRICE-005]

## 5. Temu Pivot: Semi-Managed and Local Fulfillment

### Three Operating Models (2026)

1. **Fully Managed**: Temu controls pricing, logistics, customer service. Revenue GROSS. ~20% loss rate. Pre-tariff: 90% of business. [PKT-CS-SEMIMANAGED-002, PKT-CS-DEMINIMIS-001]
2. **Semi-Managed** (launched March 2024): Merchants ship to local warehouses by sea freight. Temu handles traffic, pricing, after-sales. Generated ~$5.5B GMV (~29% of Temu total) in Q3 2025. 70% of US search traffic allocated. Profit margins improved from 2% to 4%. [PKT-CS-SEMIMANAGED-002]
3. **Third-Party Platform** (launched March 2025): Sellers independently select products, set prices, ship directly. Temu profits through commissions only. [PKT-CS-SEMIMANAGED-002]

### Warehousing Infrastructure

- 13 self-owned warehouses globally (Oct 2025): 10 Europe, 2 US, 1 Mexico border (120,000 sqm "Jitu Warehouse"). 14th planned in Rio de Janeiro. [PKT-CS-WAREHOUSE-004, PKT-INV-002]
- US locations: California (Rowland Heights/WINIT), Dallas TX, Georgia. Partners: WINIT, Easy Export as 3PL. [PKT-CS-WAREHOUSE-004]
- EU hubs: Rotterdam, Frankfurt (main). Also: UK, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Austria. Target: 80% of EU orders from local warehouses. [PKT-LOG-TEMU-EU-WAREHOUSE-001]
- Bulk shipping to UK 3PL warehouses reduced per-item logistics costs **40-60%** vs air freight. [PKT-LOG-TEMU-EU-WAREHOUSE-001]
- Pre-tariff: Temu pre-shipped 3 months of inventory to US warehouses by sea ahead of May 2 deadline. [PKT-INV-002]
- US self-operated warehouses: 15-20% of US volume (2025), projected 20-25% (2026). [PKT-CS-WAREHOUSE-004]

### Geographic GMV Shift

| Region | Share of Temu GMV | YoY Growth |
|--------|------------------|-----------|
| US | **31%** (declining) | Slowed |
| Europe | **40%** (largest) | 80-100% YoY |

[PKT-INV-007] [DATE: 2026-03] EU MAU (141.6M) now exceeds US MAU (133.6M). EU GMV projected to exceed $15B in 2025, could top $20B by end of 2026. Germany is largest EU market: 16.3M monthly recipients. [PKT-LOG-TEMU-EU-WAREHOUSE-001]

Temu Q3 2025 global GMV estimated at $22-25B, with 40-60% YoY growth. [PKT-INV-007]

## 6. Regulatory Burden Comparison

### Multi-Front Pressure on Temu (Most Exposed Globally)

**US**:
- De minimis eliminated May 2, 2025 (China/HK). Global suspension Aug 29, 2025. One Big Beautiful Bill permanently repeals by July 2027. [PKT-REG-US-001]
- 145% tariff forced 130-150% import charges. Temu pivoted to local fulfillment. [PKT-REG-US-001]
- 10 CPSC recalls. Targeted examination by CPSC since Sept 2024. New Certificates of Compliance rule effective July 8, 2026. [PKT-REG-US-002]

**EU**:
- EC preliminary DSA breach finding (July 28, 2025): Article 34 risk assessment failure. Max fine: 6% of global turnover (~$3.2B). [PKT-REG-EU-001]
- FSR dawn raid on Dublin HQ (December 10, 2025) — second FSR inspection ever. [PKT-REG-EU-002]
- EUR 3/parcel customs fee from July 1, 2026. Full de minimis elimination by 2028. [PKT-REG-EU-002]
- Poland fined Temu $1.7M (misleading pricing, Jan 2026). France eco-tax up to EUR 5/item. [PKT-REG-EU-001, PKT-REG-EU-002]
- 4.6B parcels under EUR 150 entered EU in 2024, 90%+ from China, 65%+ undervalued or split. [PKT-REG-EU-002]

**China**:
- Light treatment. Minor community group buying fines only. Supportive post-2023 stance. [PKT-REG-CN-001]

### Overall Assessment

**STRUCTURAL for Temu international; COSMETIC for Pinduoduo domestic.** The aggregate regulatory pressure has fundamentally changed Temu's cross-border model (from direct-from-China to local fulfillment). However, PDD has adapted and the Pinduoduo domestic business (majority of profits) faces minimal regulatory headwinds. Key risk: if EU DSA fine is large (up to ~$3.2B) AND FSR investigation finds distortive subsidies, the combined impact could be material. [PKT-REG-COMP-001]
