## Key Competitive Takeaways for PDD Holdings

1. **Growth deceleration is the central narrative.** PDD's revenue growth slowed from 55%+ (FY2024) to 9% (Q3 2025), while competitors like JD.com (+14.9%) and Amazon (+13%) are growing faster on a much larger base.

2. **Temu's US headwinds are real.** US MAU declined 28% YoY, and daily users fell ~48% in May 2025 following de minimis tariff elimination. However, EU (+74% MAU) and LatAm (+148% downloads) are offsetting.

3. **Douyin is the biggest domestic threat.** With 30%+ GMV growth and aggressive expansion into shelf-based e-commerce, Douyin is closing the gap with Pinduoduo in China. Douyin's GMV (~RMB 4T) is approaching Pinduoduo's (~RMB 5.2T).

4. **PDD trades at a deep discount.** Forward P/E of 8.4x vs. Alibaba (15.0x), JD (8.2-10.1x), and Amazon (26.5x). Net cash of $58 billion represents ~40% of the company's enterprise value.

5. **Amazon Haul is not yet a serious competitor.** Only 16% of US consumers shop monthly on Haul vs. 28% on Temu. But Amazon's logistics superiority (1-2 day delivery vs. 5-6 days) could shift dynamics over time.

6. **Shein and Temu face similar tariff headwinds** but different model exposures. Shein's 1P model gives more quality control; Temu's 3P model gives more scale and breadth. Both are building local fulfillment to mitigate tariff impact.
