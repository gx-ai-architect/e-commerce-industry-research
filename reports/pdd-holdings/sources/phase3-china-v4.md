## 2. Domestic Pricing (PDD vs Taobao vs JD)

Pinduoduo's average top-10 bestseller prices are **30-40% below Taobao** and **50-60% below Douyin**. PDD remains the undisputed leader in the low-price field for generic/unbranded goods. [PKT-PRICE-006]

### iPhone 17 — September 12, 2025 Launch

Pinduoduo 百亿补贴 offered up to 1,000 RMB below Apple official on day one (subsidized at 4,999 RMB). Guarantees: authentic product assurance, nationwide warranty, 10x compensation for counterfeits, double compensation if lower price found elsewhere. [PKT-PRICE-002]

### Key Pricing Insight

PDD's pricing advantage is **largest on generic/unbranded goods** (30-40% below Taobao) and **smallest on premium branded goods** where JD/Tmall match or beat via membership + national subsidy combinations. Part of PDD's low branded prices comes from **grey-market/surplus stock**, not authorized channels — a JD procurement executive quoted a brand merchant saying Pinduoduo goods "are not ours." [PKT-PRICE-006]

### National Subsidy Impact

China allocated RMB 81 billion ($11B) for consumer trade-in subsidies in 2025 (15% off phones/tablets/digital). Stackable with platform discounts on all major platforms — this **narrowed PDD's price advantage** since all platforms could offer the same government-funded discount layer. [PKT-PRICE-006]

## 3. Refund-Only Policy Reform (仅退款取消)

### Timeline

- **April 22, 2025**: All 5 major platforms (Taobao, Pinduoduo, JD, Douyin, Kuaishou) simultaneously cancelled "refund-only" default. SAMR mandated the reform — vice director Shu Wei stated SAMR summoned platforms to address rules that "squeezed merchant survival space." [PKT-REFUND-POLICY-001]

### Impact Metrics

| Metric | Before Reform | After Reform | Change |
|--------|--------------|-------------|--------|
| Malicious refund-only orders (PDD) | baseline | -62% | **-62%** |
| Merchant dispute success rate | 18% | **75%** | +57pp |
| "Arbitrary refund-only" as % of complaints | 50.36% (Q1 2025) | 37.09% (Q4 2025) | -13.3pp |
| Merchants with 80%+ refund-only rate | 7.9% | — | — |
| PDD merchant appeal success rate | <5% (pre-reform) | — | — |

[PKT-REFUND-POLICY-001] [DATE: 2025-12] Source: e-commerce industry association data, 电诉宝 platform.

### Merchant Support Program

- **April 2025**: Pinduoduo launched "千亿扶持" (RMB 100 billion) merchant support program over 3 years. Upgraded from previous "百亿减免" (10B fee reduction). Focus: demand aggregation, cost reduction, fair traffic distribution. [PKT-REFUND-POLICY-001, PKT-INV-004]

## 4. SAMR and Chinese Regulatory Environment

### PDD's Favorable Regulatory Position

- PDD became China's largest e-commerce platform by active users (March 2021) and by share value (2024), benefiting from **lighter regulatory treatment** than Alibaba. [PKT-REG-CN-001]
- Alibaba received **RMB 18.23B ($2.78B)** SAMR antitrust fine in 2021 for "pick one from two" exclusivity — the heaviest antitrust financial penalty ever imposed in China. PDD received only minor fines for community group buying. [PKT-REG-CN-001]
- SAMR total antitrust fines: RMB 22B+ across 107 decisions by end of 2024. [PKT-REG-CN-001]

### 2026 Outlook

- SAMR 2026 priorities focus on curbing "cutthroat competition" and new platform-economy regulations nearing completion. [PKT-REG-CN-001]
- Current stance is supportive of platform economy growth post-2023 relaxation. SAMR's "cutthroat competition" focus is a latent risk given PDD's aggressive pricing practices. [PKT-REG-CN-001]
- PDD 20-F risk factors cite: Anti-monopoly Law compliance, VIE structure risks, CSRC approval requirements, data security (PIPL) obligations. [PKT-REG-CN-001]

### Assessment

Severity: **COSMETIC** for Pinduoduo domestic. PDD has been a relative beneficiary of China's antitrust cycle. Alibaba's $2.78B fine weakened PDD's chief competitor. Compared to Alibaba/JD, PDD faces lighter domestic regulatory burden. [PKT-REG-CN-001]

## 6. Agricultural Strategy and C2M

### Scale

- Pinduoduo connects **16 million+ farmers** directly to consumers, bypassing traditional distribution layers. [PKT-INV-004]
- Smart Agriculture Competition (3rd edition) partnering with China Agricultural University and Zhejiang University — vertical farming challenges with LED lighting and crop modeling. [PKT-INV-004]

### C2M (Consumer-to-Manufacturer) Model

Pinduoduo's price flywheel: social incentives drive group buying -> demand clustering (团购) gives suppliers volume certainty -> factories optimize production runs -> per-unit cost compression -> higher conversion -> more merchant competition -> further price compression. Each product displays both standard price and lower group-buy price. [PKT-PRICE-006]

### Instant Retail Entry

Pinduoduo investing RMB 10B in instant retail (Duoduo Station) Sept 2025 - Sept 2026. Target: 20M daily transactions, coverage of all provincial capitals and first-tier cities by end of 2025. Investment covers infrastructure upgrades, software development, consumer/merchant subsidies, external delivery driver collaboration. [PKT-LOG-PDD-DOMESTIC-001]
