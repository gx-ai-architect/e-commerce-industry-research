## 5. VIE Structure & Revenue Recognition

### VIE Revenue Share Decline

| Year | VIE Share of Total Revenue |
|------|--------------------------|
| FY2022 | 56.2% |
| FY2023 | 45.7% |
| FY2024 | 22.5% |

[DATE: 2025-04-28] From 20-F. **CRITICAL**: This does NOT mean Temu is 77.5% of the business. (PKT-BM-004)

### Gross vs Net Revenue Recognition

- **Pinduoduo (domestic)**: Pure marketplace. Merchants set prices. Revenue recognized **NET** (commissions + ad fees only). A RMB 100 PDD order contributes ~RMB 4-5 to revenue.
- **Temu (international)**: Fully-managed/consignment model. Platform sets consumer prices, handles logistics. Revenue recognized **GROSS** (full selling price). A RMB 100 Temu order contributes ~RMB 100 to revenue.
- Semi-managed Temu model (29% of Temu GMV in Q3 2025): Revenue recognition closer to NET.

The VIE share declining reflects Temu's rapid scaling with gross recognition, not a proportional domestic/international GMV shift. (PKT-BM-004)

### VIE Risk

PDD Holdings Inc. (Cayman Islands) does NOT have any equity ownership in the VIE or its subsidiaries. Control through contractual arrangements (WFOE agreements). PRC subsidiaries can only receive funding through capital contributions or loans, subject to government approval. (PKT-BM-004)
