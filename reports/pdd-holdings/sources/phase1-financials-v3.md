## 9. Tariff & Trade Policy Impact on Temu

### Timeline
- **Feb 2025**: Trump executive orders imposed 25% tariffs on Canada/Mexico imports, additional 10% on China; initially revoked de minimis exemption, then paused
- **Apr 25, 2025**: Temu began imposing ~145% import charges on US orders; $14 sneakers became $27
- **May 2, 2025**: De minimis exemption officially closed for Chinese shipments; duties up to 120% or flat $100 fee (rising to $200 in June)
- **Apr 10, 2025**: PDD stock hit 52-week low of $87.11 amid tariff panic

### Impact
- Temu lost 58% of US daily active users post-tariff
- Cargo shipments from China reduced by up to 60%
- Temu pivoted to local warehouse/semi-managed model (~20%+ of US GMV by end-2024 per Citi)
- Strategy shift: goods shipped in bulk to US warehouses, then distributed domestically

### Analyst Views
- **Citi**: "New tariffs will still have a negative read-through to Temu's growth in 2025 and beyond"
- **Coresight Research**: "Chinese e-commerce apps are very adept and agile. They have contingency plans in place"
- **Seeking Alpha**: PDD can maintain price advantage via direct-to-consumer consignment model
- **Legal**: A US federal court order vacated parts of Trump's executive order; de minimis exemption status remains fluid

## 10. Key Risk Factors (from 20-F FY2024)

1. **VIE Structure**: PDD is a Cayman Islands holding company; operates in China via VIE. Cash held by VIE/China subsidiaries may not be available for use outside mainland China
2. **Regulatory Risk**: PRC Anti-monopoly Law, CSRC oversight, CAC cybersecurity reviews; failure to obtain permissions could result in fines/restrictions
3. **Data Privacy**: Reports of Pinduoduo app exploiting Android vulnerabilities for unauthorized user data access; securities class action filed Aug 2024 (E.D.N.Y.)
4. **Trade Policy**: De minimis closure and 145% tariffs on Chinese imports directly impact Temu's US business model
5. **Competition**: Intense domestic competition from JD.com, Alibaba, Douyin; international competition from Amazon, Shein
6. **No Capital Return**: No buyback or dividend despite $59.5B cash pile; management explicitly deferred capital return
